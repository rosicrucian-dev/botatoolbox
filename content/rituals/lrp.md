## Qabalistic Cross

i. Touching the forehead, say [Ateh](/words-of-power/athh) (Unto Thee).
ii. Touching the breast, say [Malkuth](/words-of-power/mlkvth) (The Kingdom).
iii. Touching the right shoulder, say [ve-Geburah](/words-of-power/vgbvrh) (and the Power).
iv. Touching the left shoulder, say [ve-Gedulah](/words-of-power/vgdvlh) (and the Glory).
v. Clasping the hands upon the breast, say [le-Olahm](/words-of-power/lovlm), [Amen](/words-of-power/amn) (to the Ages, Amen).

## Formulation of the Pentagrams

vi. Turning to the East, make a Pentagram (that of Earth). Say [IHVH](/words-of-power/ihvh).
vii. Turning to the South, the same, but say [ADNI](/words-of-power/adni).
viii. Turning to the West, the same, but say [AHIH](/words-of-power/ahih).
ix. Turning to the North, the same, but say [AGLA](/words-of-power/agla).
x. Extending the arms in the form of a Cross, say:
xi. Before me [Raphael](/words-of-power/rpal);
xii. Behind me [Gabriel](/words-of-power/gbrial);
xiii. On my right hand [Michael](/words-of-power/mikal);
xiv. On my left hand [Auriel](/words-of-power/avrial);
xv. Before me flames the Pentagram;
xvi. Behind me shines the six-rayed Star.
